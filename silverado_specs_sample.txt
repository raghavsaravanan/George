SECTION 03: ENGINE MECHANICAL - INTAKE MANIFOLD INSTALLATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FASTENER TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS:
================================================================================
Application                     | Metric Units    | English Units
================================================================================
Intake Manifold Bolts - First Pass| 3 N·m           | 27 lb-in
Intake Manifold Bolts - Final Pass| 12 N·m          | 106 lb-in
Fuel Rail Fasteners (M6)        | 10 N·m          | 89 lb-in
Throttle Body Bolts             | 10 N·m          | 89 lb-in
MAP Sensor Bolt                 | 6 N·m           | 53 lb-in
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CAUTION: Do not confuse inch-pounds (lb-in) with foot-pounds (lb-ft). Applying 106 lb-ft of torque to the intake manifold bolts will result in catastrophic failure of the aluminum cylinder head threads.
